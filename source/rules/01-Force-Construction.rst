Force Construction
------------------------------------

The *Force Construction* rules for `Outworlds Wastes <https://outworlds-wastes.jeremylt.org/force-construction>`_ are fully replaced with these rules.

* Commanders have a modified `Danais <https://www.sarna.net/wiki/Danais_(DropShip_class)>`_ class DropShip with a single 'Mech bay. Additional bay space may be added later as part of :doc:`Force Management </rules/02-Force-Management>`.

* Commanders may select any single 'Mech of up to 1.800 BV from any faction or era. This 'Mech will be the primary unit in their force, so commanders are recommended to consider several factors, including tactical flexibility and maintainability.

* The 1.800 BV limit does not include the pilot skill level. The pilot for this 'Mech starts at Gunnery 3/Piloting 4 and can be *trained* later.

* If the unit is extinct in the ilClan era, then the cost to *repair* and *replace* this unit is 10% higher.
